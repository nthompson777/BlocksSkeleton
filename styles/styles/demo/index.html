Adjustments to Living Expense Amount(s)

This section allows you to create varying expense streams in the Retirement period. You would use this section if you have:

  • A significant portion of your Living Expenses that are fixed (versus inflating) expenses.
  • Expenses that will end during retirement.

Indicate an expense that is "fixed" by entering the appropriate monthly amount and leaving Inflation set to No. If this fixed expense will end during retirement, enter the year the expense will end. If this fixed expense will continue throughout the Analysis, enter any year that is later than the End of the Analysis.

Indicate an expense that will end during retirement by entering the appropriate monthly amount and entering the year the expense will end. If this expense is fixed, leave Inflation set to No. If this expense will inflate, select Yes.

One common fixed expense is a mortgage. The two examples below show the input and the results for, first, a mortgage that ends during retirement, and, second, a mortgage that continues through the end of the Analysis. The Annual Living Expense column shows the value that would be displayed in the Combined Details Chart.

Example 1 - Client retired; Living Expense (LE) of $5,000 per month; inflation is 5%; Client Life is 2016; mortgage is $1,000 per month and will end in 2014.
